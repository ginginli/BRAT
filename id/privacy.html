Introduction

Welcome to Brat Generator. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing you with a safe experience. We may collect information that you provide directly to us when using our Site. Additionally, we automatically collect certain information about your device when you visit our Site.

Information You Provide to Us

We may collect information that you provide when you use our Site, including:

  • Contact information, such as your name and email address, if you choose to contact us.
  • Any other information you choose to provide.

Information We Collect Automatically

When you visit our Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • Usage Information: We collect information about your interactions with our Site, including the pages or content you view, your browser type, and the referring webpage or source which may have sent you to our Site.
  • Device Information: We collect information about the device you use to access our Site, including the hardware model, operating system and version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Log Information: Our servers automatically record information including your IP address, browser type, referring/exit pages, operating system, date/time stamps, and clickstream data.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your browsing behaviors and to help provide functionality to the Site.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Site and services;

  • Carry out any other purpose for which the information was collected.
