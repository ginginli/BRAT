Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download the generated image?

You can click the "Save Image" button to download the generated image, or take a screenshot to save it.

Can I use the generated images for commercial purposes?

Images generated are available for personal use. For commercial use, please contact us for permission.

Why is my text not displaying completely?

Text length is limited based on the theme. Green and blue themes are limited to 20 characters, while black and white themes are limited to 100 characters.

Can I customize the background image?

Currently, we offer four preset themes. Custom background functionality will be available in future updates.
